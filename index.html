Crafting: Doll Wire Frame Work

“Crafting is a great way to take your mind off of your worries. Scrolling on your favorite social media app is a very common way to distract yourself. But unfortunately that can lead to long periods of time disappearing and you being left feeling unproductive and guilty. Crafting is just as much of a time consumer. Having a tangible object as a result of your time, somehow feels very satisfying. The actual act of using your hands to create something can be therapeutic. It is a way of turning off a certain part of the brain and giving it a break. When it comes to school there are many things you are learning, and maybe struggling to learn. But having the feeling of accomplishment from working on a crafting project gives you that vote of confidence to put you in a positive headspace. So when you go back to your original stressor, you are going in with a refreshed mindset. Here are some links on how to get started on making your own doll!”

Exercising

“Exercise is a powerful antidote to stress, acting like a pressure release valve for your body's built-up tension. When you work out, your brain releases endorphins, natural mood lifters that counteract stress hormones like cortisol. Physical activity also stimulates the production of serotonin and dopamine, boosting your mood and promoting relaxation. Beyond the chemical benefits, focusing on a workout provides a welcome distraction from daily worries, giving your mind a break. Plus, achieving fitness goals, big or small, can enhance your confidence and sense of accomplishment. Exercising strengthens your body and equips you with a vital tool for managing stress more effectively.”
